# Principles


#### Routines

Follow from the "You must" principle. Make it a habit, so doing otherwise takes effort.


1. Brainstorm who you would have yourself be
2. Decide who you will be and simplify it to the most important aspects
3. Determine what that person **must** do
4. Teach yourself doing what you must is what brings you happiness
5. Have to find joy in doing those things, not their results
6. In every moment of your life, do everything you must immediately
7. Relax, rest, and explore whenever that must be done is done

#### Problems

* Want to do everything
* Distractions by good things that are not most important
* Improving without finishing (publishing, productionalizing, sharing, etc.)
* Daydreaming
* Waiting for tomorrow
* Balance between being overly dramatic or not taking it seriously enough
* Not taking pride in who you would have yourself be
* Being tired, hungover, or in a bad mood
* Being dragged away by friends or family
* Being distracted by work
* Not having the means, money, time, etc.

#### Reminders

* It's okay to do the easier of the "musts" as long as it's a must
