
# RFC 000: Farming 

Problem to solve

Actors need some purpose.

Give the occupation of a farming.
Where to put the farm.
A goal.
Steps along the way.
Modify world.



### Dependencies

* Actors having plans
* `Occupation` cards
* `Strategy` cards
* Voxel have a type
* Terraforming to flatten the land
* Cost estimate of where to place the farm
* Some way to protect the farm from being change to something else



### Design

`Occupation > Strategy > Plan > Phase > Task`

An Actor gets an `Occupation`. This defines what kind of Strategies they are interested in. Strategies are long-term plans.  An Actor likely has 0-3 of these. They may last the entire lifetime of the Actor.  An Actor my abandon, change, or adopt a Stratgey but this is rare an "life chaning event" in the Actor's world.

The `Strategy` is composed on a set of smaller `Plan`s. Theses are larger objectives, but more malleable to the current conditions of the world. It's much more likely that external circumstances may cause Plans to be abandoned, modified, reset, or otherwise changed.

A `Phase` is a concrete sequence of Tasks.

A `Task` is "atomic" unit of change.
