## Vision and Mission

Tangle Orchard Sutdios is a creative development studio aiming to create a profitable, sustainable company on top of open source development. The focus of our development is on Kestrel, a infinite-world adventure game based both in the heritage of tabeltop role-playing games as well as modern open world gaming.


Another passion of the studio is _productivity_. We try to open source the tools (whether software or processes and habits!) as well on our journey to build Tangled Orchard.

Our core values are:

* Sharing knowledge and sharing enjoyment are paramount
* There are more important things than work
* Productivity is enormously important if it helps us become the people we want to be

Our business model is as follows:

* Write our software as open source
* Sell packaged software for a reasonable price
* Release content expansions in advance to paying users
* Provide a venue for contributors to market and sell expansions

Our timeline

* July 2020: a first version of the Tangled Orchard Studios was made public: asymbolic step of moving the development of Kestrel from being a hobby project to the foundation of a future company.
