
# Characters


## Contentment

Contentment is the "core" attribute of a character in Kestrel.

It is a measure of how well a character's expectations in are being met.  In some cases, this is can be thought of as a measurement of happiness, but not in all. A prisoner, enprisoned since childhood may find "contentment" that would still be a far cry from what might objectively be called happiness.  Thus, the character's expectations are play a key role in the determination of a character's contentment.

In general, a character's contentment is determined by:

* The successes or failures of their [Strategies](strategy), [Plans](plan), and [Tasks](task)
* Events and encounters that align to their [Values](value) and [Trigger](value)
