# Occupation

A character's **occupation** is literally how the character "occupies their time."  The could be, but is not necessarily, their profession.  Example occupations:

* Farmer
* Adventurer
* Alcoholic
* Vagabond

The occupation guides which [Strategies](strategy) the character adopts (since the Strategy is what they will be spending most of their time pursuing when not distracted by reactive events).

Strategies are then pursued via a series of Plans. Plans are in turn composed of Tasks.


## Future Directions

**Multiple occupations**.  Characters may have a profession as well as a hobby. They may have a daytime occupation as well as evening occupation.  They may have multiple occupations with rules about which one decides their actions.
